AboutUs

Why Choose us?

Senda's Siakoy House embarked on its flavorful journey seven years ago, with a humble beginning rooted in a family's love for traditional Filipino delicacies. From the onset, they dedicated themselves to crafting siakoy, butchi, and binangkal that captured the essence of homemade goodness. As the years unfolded, Senda's Siakoy House evolved with a commitment to innovation and customer satisfaction. Embracing the spirit of culinary creativity, they expanded their menu to include tempting additions like caramel, chocomallow, and yema. These new offerings became instant favorites, reflecting the family's dedication to enhancing their culinary repertoire while preserving the authentic taste that patrons have come to cherish.


Mission

At Sendas Siakoy House, we passionately prepare and serve authentic siakoy, binangkal, and butchi while curating a selection of premium beauty products. Our aim is to create moments of indulgence and self-care, delivering exceptional culinary delights and curated experiences to every patron we serve. 


Vission

To be the premier destination for authentic Filipino delicacies and curated beauty products, offering a harmonious blend of culinary excellence and self-indulgence.
